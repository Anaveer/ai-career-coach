# AI Career Coach 🚀

Welcome to the AI Career Coach! This is a smart web application that helps you analyze your resume and prepare for technical interviews. It acts like an expert recruiter to give you honest, AI-powered feedback.

## What does it do?
* **Resume Scoring:** Upload your PDF resume, type your target job role, and the AI will give you a matching score (out of 100%).
* **Finds Missing Skills:** It tells you exactly which skills you are missing for that job and provides direct YouTube and Documentation links to learn them.
* **Action Plan:** Gives you 3 simple steps to improve your chances of getting hired.
* **Mock Interview Chatbox:** A smart assistant where you can practice technical interview questions and get proper, code-backed answers.
* **PDF Report:** You can easily download your entire analysis as a clean, professional PDF.

## Technologies Used
* **Frontend:** HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Vanilla)
* **AI Power:** Groq Cloud API (Llama 3.3 Model)
* **PDF Reading:** PDF.js Library
* **Markdown Formatting:** Marked.js

## How to Use
1. Open the website.
2. Select your experience level and type your target job (e.g., Backend Developer).
3. Upload your Resume in PDF format.
4. Click "Analyze Resume" and wait for the AI to generate your personalized report.
5. Use the Chatbox on the right to practice any questions from the report!
